Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects take 2–4 weeks depending on the scope. We always provide a timeline before we start.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! All our packages include at least 2 rounds of revisions. We want to make sure you love the final result.

What if I don’t know exactly what I need?

No worries! We’ll guide you through a quick discovery process to clarify your goals and suggest the best solution.

Can you work with my existing brand?

Absolutely. If you have an existing logo, palette, or voice, we’ll incorporate them into your new site seamlessly.

Do you build on WordPress or just custom code?

We do both! Whether you prefer WordPress for content control or need a custom-coded solution, we’ve got you covered.

Is SEO included?

All our builds follow SEO best practices by default. Includes mobile responsiveness & fast load times, and clean structure.
